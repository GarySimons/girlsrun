Find the right gear

Shoes

These are the single most important piece of your running kit. If possible, invest in the best pair that you can afford. Your legs will thank you for this! The most important part of any running shoe is comfort. Ideally you should visit a sports shop where you can be advised on the correct pair for your style of running. Have a test in the shop by running up and down, hop and jump to feel how the shoes are when moving. You risk injury and discomfort if you don’t get the right shoes.

Sports Bra

You need to invest in a good sports bra. If you are a A or B cup, a compression bra will be best. A C cup would be best with an encapsulation bra. If you are a D cup or above, you’ll need a bra that has both compression and encapsulation.

Clothing

You can run in whatever makes you feel the most comfortable, but be aware that some fabrics can retain a lot of moisture or have seams that could rub. There are lots of specialist running clothing on the market that will help to keep you comfortable and less hot and sweaty. These may improve your overall running experience.

Safety

If you are going to be running in the dark, it’s vital that you invest in some high visibility clothing to keep you seen and safe. There are many products around from armbands to full reflective jackets and leggings. It’s also important to be seen in the daytime too. Wear bright colours that will stand out and get you noticed.

Playlist

Many runners love the quiet and solitude of running. But lots love to run to upbeat music to keep pace and motivation, or podcast and books to keep them company along the way. Whatever you choose, always make sure you are aware of your surroundings and any dangers that there might be.
