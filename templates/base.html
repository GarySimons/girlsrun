What we do

GROUP RUNS

Girls on the run offers women to take part in women only runs around brighton and Susex. We plan the routes to take in some of the most beautiful landscapes and locations that our area has to offer. All our runs are suitable for runners of all abilities. We aim to be inclusive, supportive and social. Join us on one of our runs and find out the massive benefits of running with a group.

ADVICE

Our memembers enjoy a wide range of advice, from starting as a non-runner through to experienced veterans. On registering on this website, you'll gain access to all the profeesional advice you need. From choosing kit, eating right to helping your body to take you further. We all need a bit of a helping hand sometimes. You can also sign up for bespoke coaching and nutrition plans with one-on-one service.

My Story

How I found running, and myself

I started this group out of a desire to help women like me start running and discover the benefits that it can bring. My mission is to provide a group that is based on women supporting and empowering each other. I believe that it should be fun and encouraging, so women can relax and push themselves to do more than they believed they could.

All our runs are designed around being social, supportive, inclusive and suitable for all runners. I’ve found some great local routes taking us to some of Sussex’s most beautiful and interesting locations.

Please sign up to our membership to access special coaching and nutrition advice and bespoke plans to help you get the best out of yourself.
