What we do

GROUP RUNS

Girls on the Run offers women a chance to take part in women only runs around Brighton and Sussex. We plan the routes to take in some of the most beautiful landscapes and locations that our area has to offer. All our runs are suitable for runners of all abilities. We are inclusive, supportive and social. Join us for a runs and find out the massive benefits of running with a group

MEMBER EXTRAS

Our members enjoy a wide range of advice, from starting as a non-runner to experienced veterans. On registering on this website, you'll gain access to all the professional advice you need. From choosing kit to helping your body to take you further. We all need a bit of a helping hand sometimes. You can also sign up for bespoke coaching and nutrition plans with one-on-one service.
