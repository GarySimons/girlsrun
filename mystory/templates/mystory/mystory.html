My Story 2

How I found running, and myself

I must start by confessing that I used to hate running. I found it hard, boring and could believe that you could ever make it enjoyable. Starting to run was a massive achievement for me and has taken me on a fantastic journey. I have improved both my fitness levels and my mental health, and made some amazing friends along the way.

I started this group out of a desire to help women like me start running and discover the benefits that it can bring. My mission is to provide a group that is based on women supporting and empowering each other. I believe that it should be fun and encouraging, so women can relax and push themselves to do more than they believed they could.

All our runs are designed around being social, supportive, inclusive and suitable for all runners. I’ve found some great local routes taking us to some of Sussex’s most beautiful and interesting locations.

Please sign up to our membership to access special coaching and nutrition advice and bespoke plans to help you get the best out of yourself.
